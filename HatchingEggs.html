Hatching Eggs:

  • We deal exclusively in Broiler Hatching eggs of Breed Cobb400/Cobb 400Y.
  • All eggs are produced in Environment controlled conditions and temperature is mantained between 58˚F to 62˚F.
  • The age of the eggs vary from 32 weeks to 58 weeks.
  • Transportation of eggs takes place via Refrigerated vans to ensure the quality of the product.
  • These eggs provide excellent hatchability.
  • The chicks have excellent lifespan after hatching.
  • Our farms are currently working at the capacity of 1.25 lakhs with annual yield of 2crore, 50 lakhs.
